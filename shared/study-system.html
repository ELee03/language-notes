Japanese Grammar

Method

Study System

A low-friction loop for keeping language learning alive.

Daily Loop

Start with grammar as the decoder, then use Anki as memory support, then parse real sentences from media. The goal is not to finish a deck; the goal is to understand increasingly real Japanese.

Normal day: 15 minutes grammar, 10-15 minutes Anki, 10-15 minutes media or shadowing.

Dialogue Parsing Order

Find the final predicate. Identify particles. Notice omitted context. Recognize the sentence ending. Use Korean to confirm structure. Check English last.

Anki Rule

Cap Anki before it eats the whole routine. If reviews exceed the cap for three days, set new cards to zero and keep contact with media anyway.
